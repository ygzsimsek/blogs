## Crafting the Perfect AI Wallpaper Prompt

Creating a wallpaper that’s so you starts with mastering the prompt. Think of it as describing your ideal scene in a way that an AI can bring to life. Here are a few pro tips to make sure your prompts are clear, creative, and get the results you’re after!

- **Be Specific**: Instead of just saying “flower wallpaper,” try “vibrant 4K wildflower field with mountains in the background at sunrise.” 
- **Add Styles or Moods**: Words like “minimalist,” “vintage,” or “cyberpunk” can dramatically change the outcome.
- **Experiment with Keywords**: Don’t be afraid to add details like colors, lighting (e.g., “soft, warm glow”), and even composition (e.g., “centered, with blurred background”).
