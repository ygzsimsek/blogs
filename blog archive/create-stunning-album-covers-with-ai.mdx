## Best Practices for AI Album Cover Design

To make your album cover stand out:
- **Keep It Simple**: Avoid overcrowding the design. A clear, bold image is more impactful.
- **Match the Genre**: Align your design elements with your music style. For example, soft pastels for lo-fi beats or dark tones for metal.
- **Focus on Typography**: Your album name and artist title should be legible and complement the design.
