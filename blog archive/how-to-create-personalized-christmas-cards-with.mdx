## Tips for Designing the Perfect Christmas Card

- **Be Specific:** When entering prompts, include details like colors, themes, and styles.
- **Choose the Right Font:** Fonts like script or serif can add elegance and festivity.
- **Experiment:** Don’t hesitate to try different prompts or layouts to find the perfect design.
- **Think About the Recipient:** Tailor your card’s design and message to the person receiving it.
