## Creating Your Own Playing Deck

### Part 1: Create a New Project

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-dashboard.png"
    alt="creating a new project"
/>

Start by creating a new project in Stockimg.ai's user-friendly interface. Choose the option to design a playing deck and set the dimensions according to your preferred card size.

### Part 2: Select Illusion Category and Vector Art Model

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/poker-deck-aces.png"
    alt="navigating to categories"
/>

Navigate to the **Illusion** category in dashboard. Select the **Vector Art** model to ensure crisp and scalable designs for your playing cards. 

Alternatively, you can experiment with other categories and models for your taste.

> If you want to learn more about the models and categories, check out our guide on [Stockimg.ai's Categories and Models](https://stockimg.ai/blog/guide/stockimg-categories-and-models-explained).

### Part 3: Creation and Design

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/aces.png"
    alt="aces designs"
/>

Explore the various canvas options and prompts available within Stockimg.ai to kickstart your design process. Experiment with different styles, colors, and themes to create a cohesive and visually appealing deck.

> For this project, we used the prompt: "ace of spades, card deck, vector art, white background, black, white, red, tattoo design, simple design, minimalist design". Feel free to adjust to prompt anyway you like!

**Creating other Cards**

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/other-cards.png"
    alt="other cards"
/>

To create the other cards of the deck, we adjust the prompt to create cards like Kings, Queens, Jacks, and others.

> Pro Tip: Use "Remove Background" and "Upscale" options for the best results!

**Designing the Back of the Deck**

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/deck-back.png"

You can also create your own uniqe design for the back of your deck!

### Part 4: Export Your Designs

Once you're satisfied with your designs, export them in your preferred format, whether it's PNG, JPG, SVG, or PDF. Stockimg.ai offers flexible export options to ensure compatibility with your printing and publishing needs.

> Pro Tip: You can export as PDF to directly print your uniqe deck!
