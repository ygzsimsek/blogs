## How to Create Your Wallpaper Using Stockimg AI

Designing a wallpaper with Stockimg AI is as easy as following these steps:

### 1. Define Your Vibe
Think about the mood or theme you want for your wallpaper. Do you want something calming, bold, or futuristic? Jot down keywords like "serene forest," "cyberpunk city," or "pastel abstract."

### 2. Input Your Prompt
Head to the wallpaper generator on **Stockimg.ai** and enter your chosen prompt. Be as specific as you can! For example:
- "4K sunset over a calm ocean, pastel tones, and dreamy lighting."
- "Cyberpunk cityscape at night with neon reflections, ultra-detailed."

### 3. Preview and Adjust
Stockimg AI will generate a preview based on your input. If it’s not quite right, tweak the keywords or style until it perfectly fits your vision.

### 4. Download and Apply
Once satisfied, download your design in high resolution. Apply it as your wallpaper, and you’re done!

> **Pro Tip**: Use descriptive words like "vivid," "soft glow," or "geometric patterns" to refine your output further.
