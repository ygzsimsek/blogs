## Step-by-Step Guide to Designing a Book Cover with Stockimg.ai

### Step 1: Sign Up or Sign In and Access the Dashboard
![stockimg dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/sai-dashboard-home.png)

- Start by logging into your Stockimg.ai account. Once you’ve signed in, head over to the **Dashboard** to begin designing your book cover.

### Step 2: Choose the Book Cover Category
![stockimg categories](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/sai-dashboard-categories.png)

- Navigate to the left side of the dashboard and click on the **Book Cover** category. This section is specifically designed to help you create eye-catching covers for any genre.

### Step 3: Enter Your Prompt or Choose From Example Book Covers
![stockimg book cover gen](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/sai-bc-gen.png)

- **Write Your Prompt**: Describe the cover you're envisioning. For example: “A mysterious, dark fantasy cover with a glowing ancient sword in the foreground.”
- **Select from Examples**: You can also browse through example book covers for inspiration or select one as a starting point for your design.

### Step 4: Customize Your Book Cover
![stockimg book cover edit](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/sai-bc-edit.png)

- **Edit with AI**: Use the chat-style prompt box to request specific changes in your cover. For instance:
    - “Add a red sky with storm clouds.”
    - “Make the title text larger and bolder.”
    - “What if the sword was replaced with a magical staff?”

- **Additional Editing Tools**:
    - **Retry**: Regenerate the image for a new design.
    - **AI Upscale 4x**: Increase the resolution of your book cover for high-quality printing or digital use.
    - **AI Remove Background**: Remove the background to focus on specific elements.
    - **Change Style**: Explore different cover styles, such as vintage, minimalist, or modern.
    - **Resize**: Adjust the cover dimensions to fit paperback, hardcover, or eBook formats.
    - **Undo/Redo**: Use the undo/redo buttons to quickly reverse or reapply changes.

### Step 5: Download or Share Your Book Cover
![book cover made with stockimg](https://media.stockimg.ai/template/image/YJ4AgvVlgzIt.png)

- Once you’re happy with your book cover design, click **Download** to save it for use, or generate a **Share Link** to show it online.

### Extra Step: Use the Canvas for Advanced Customization
![sai canvas](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/sai-canvas.png)

- **Canvas**: For additional precision, use the **Canvas** feature to take control over individual design elements. You can:
    - **Move and Resize Objects**: Perfect your layout by repositioning or resizing objects.
    - **Layering**: Manage the depth of each component by adjusting the layering order.
    - **Add Text or Shapes**: Enhance your cover with custom text, shapes, or any additional design elements.
    
With Stockimg.ai’s intuitive design tools and advanced Canvas features, creating professional book covers has never been easier!
