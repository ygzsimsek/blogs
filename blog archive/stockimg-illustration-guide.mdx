## Step-by-Step Guide to Designing Illustrations with Stockimg.ai

## Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Illustrations with Stockimg.ai

### Step 1: Sign Up or Sign In and Access the Dashboard
![stockimg dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-dashboard-home.png)

- Begin by logging into your Stockimg.ai account and accessing the **Dashboard** to start working on your custom illustration.

### Step 2: Choose the Illustration Category
![stockimg categories](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-dashboard-categories.png)

- Navigate to the left panel of the dashboard and select the **Illustration** category. This section allows you to create everything from detailed character designs to playful scenes.

### Step 3: Enter Your Prompt or Choose From Example Illustrations
![stockimg generation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-illustration-gen.png)

- **Write Your Prompt**: Describe the illustration you want to generate. For example: “A playful cartoon of a cat chasing a butterfly in a colorful garden.”
- **Explore Example Illustrations**: If you need some inspiration, browse through the example illustrations gallery, and pick one to start your design.

### Step 4: Customize Your Illustration
![editing illustrations with ai](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-illustration-edit.png)

- **Edit with AI**: Use the chat-style prompt box to refine your illustration in real-time. For instance, you can type:
    - “Make the cat’s fur orange.”
    - “Change the background to a sunset sky.”
    - “What if the scene were more minimalist?”

- **Additional Editing Tools**:
    - **Retry**: Regenerate the image if you want a completely new version.
    - **AI Upscale 4x**: Enhance the resolution of your illustration for sharper details.
    - **AI Remove Background**: Remove the background if you want to isolate the characters or objects.
    - **Change Style**: Experiment with different art styles, such as flat design, line art, or watercolor.
    - **Resize**: Adjust the dimensions of your illustration to fit your specific needs.
    - **Undo/Redo**: Quickly reverse or retry any changes you’ve made with the undo and redo buttons.

### Step 5: Download or Share Your Illustration
![illustration example made with stockimg](https://media.stockimg.ai/designed/q2ITl-hUc_IA.png)

- Once your illustration is exactly how you envisioned it, click **Download** to save the file, or use the **Share Link** feature to display your work online.

### Extra Step: Use the Canvas for Full Control Over Your Illustration
![stockimg canvas](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-canvas.png)

- **Canvas**: For even more precision, use the **Canvas** feature to make manual adjustments to individual elements. With the Canvas, you can:
    - **Move and Resize Objects**: Rearrange elements within your illustration for perfect composition.
    - **Layering**: Control the depth of objects by adjusting the layering of each component.
    - **Add Text or Shapes**: Further enhance your illustration by adding custom text, shapes, or design elements.

With the Canvas and AI editing tools, Stockimg.ai offers complete creative freedom, making illustration design easier, more flexible, and more fun.
