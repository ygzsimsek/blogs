### Tips for Designing Effective Mobile Icons

- **Keep It Simple**: Simplicity is key for mobile icons since they need to be recognizable at small sizes.
- **Focus on Contrast**: Use high contrast between your icon’s elements and background to ensure visibility.
- **Ensure Scalability**: Test your icon at various sizes to make sure it looks clear and professional, whether it’s displayed in an app store or on a mobile screen.
- **Match App Identity**: Design the icon to reflect the core function and personality of your app.
