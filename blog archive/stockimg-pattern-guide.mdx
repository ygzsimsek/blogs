## What You Can Create with Stockimg.ai's Pattern Generator

![a miserable man painting the wall](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/gibi-duvar.png)

AI-generated patterns have a wide range of applications, allowing designers to use them for both functional and creative purposes. Here are some key uses:

1. **Packaging Design**: Whether you’re working on retail packaging or eco-friendly designs, AI-generated patterns add an extra layer of brand personality to product wrapping, boxes, and labels.

2. **Game Assets**: For game developers, AI-generated patterns are essential for creating textures that can be used repeatedly across environments. From terrain textures to character clothing, patterns play a key role in visual consistency.

3. **Wallpapers**: Both physical wallpaper and digital wallpapers (for websites or apps) rely heavily on AI-generated patterns to cover large areas without visible breaks or inconsistencies.

4. **Backgrounds for Websites**: Patterns offer a subtle way to enhance the visual appeal of a website while maintaining readability. They can serve as a backdrop for content, giving web pages a cohesive design.

5. **Fabric and Textile Design**: Designers in the fashion and interior design industries can create fabric prints for clothing, curtains, and upholstery. AI-generated patterns ensure that the design repeats flawlessly, no matter the fabric size.

6. **Stationery and Office Supplies**: Customize notepads, folders, and business stationery with attractive, repeating patterns. Patterns add a polished look to everyday items and make brands stand out.

7. **Marketing**: Use patterns in brochures, posters, and flyers to create consistent and eye-catching designs. Patterns can complement a brand's aesthetic and make printed materials more engaging.

![choosing your category](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-dashboard-categories.png)

### Step 3: Enter a Design Prompt or Choose From Example Patterns
![creating the mobile icon](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-pattern-gen.png)

- You can either:
    - **Enter a detailed design prompt** describing the type of pattern you want (e.g., “floral pattern with pastel colors” or “geometric shapes with a modern style”).
    - **Choose from pre-existing pattern templates** provided in the gallery if you prefer to start from a pre-made option.

### Step 4: Customize Your Pattern Design
![customizing the mobile icon](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-pattern-edit.png)

- Once your pattern is generated, you can begin **customizing** it:

- For advanced customization, you can use the **Canvas Tool**, which provides a flexible workspace to manually adjust elements, layer new components, and refine spacing between pattern elements.
