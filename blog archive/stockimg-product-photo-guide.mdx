## How to Create Product Photos With AI

### Step 1: Sign Up or Sign In and Access the Dashboard
![sai home](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-dashboard-home.png)

- Log in to your Stockimg.ai account and navigate to the **Dashboard** to get started.

### Step 2: Choose the Product Photo Category
![choosing your category](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-dashboard-categories.png)

- From the sidebar, select the **Product Photo** category. This section is tailored for creating high-quality product images that enhance your listings.

### Step 3: Enter Your Product Details
![creating the product](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-product-gen.png)

- In the prompt box, provide detailed information about your product. You can specify attributes such as:
    - Product type (e.g., clothing, electronics, furniture)
    - Color and texture
    - Desired background setting (e.g., plain, lifestyle shot)

### Step 4: Customize Your Product Photo
![customizing the product](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-product-edit.png)

- **Edit with AI**: Make adjustments using prompts like:
    - “Add a shadow effect.”
    - “Change the background to a light wood texture.”
    - “What if it were on a model?”

- **Additional Editing Features**:
    - **Retry**: Regenerate the image to try different variations.
    - **AI Remove Background**: Create a clean product image with a transparent background.
    - **Change Style**: Experiment with various photographic styles, such as studio lighting or natural light.
    - **Resize**: Modify the dimensions for different platforms or marketing materials.
    - **Undo/Redo**: Easily manage any changes you make during the customization process.

### Step 5: Download or Share Your Product Photo
![final products](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-product-bottle.png)

- Once you’re satisfied with the final design, click **Download** to save your high-resolution product photo, or generate a **Share Link** to promote it online.

Additionally, use can use the advanced canvas features to create a beatiful visual to post on your social media. Even better, you can now automize your social media accounts!

## Tips for Best Results

- **High-Resolution Photos:** Start with the highest resolution photo possible. This gives the AI more data to work with and results in a better final image.

- **Consistent Lighting:** Ensure your product is evenly lit to avoid harsh shadows or bright spots.

- **Neutral Background:** A plain background helps the AI focus on the product itself, making the enhancement process more effective.
