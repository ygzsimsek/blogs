## Step-by-Step Guide to Designing a Wallpaper with Stockimg.ai

### Step 1: Sign Up or Sign In and Access the Dashboard
![sai home](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-dashboard-home.png)

- After signing in, head to the dashboard to begin your wallpaper creation.

### Step 2: Choose the Wallpaper Category
![choosing your category](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-dashboard-categories.png)

- On the dashboard, click on the **Wallpaper** category to start designing. You’ll see a range of example wallpapers in the gallery, or you can jump right in by entering your own prompt.

### Step 3: Enter Your Prompt or Select From Examples
![creating the wallpaer](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-wp-gen.png)

- In the prompt box, describe your vision for the wallpaper. You can type something like “Mountain landscape at sunset” or “Abstract art with vibrant colors.” Alternatively, choose an image from the example gallery above to spark inspiration.

### Step 4: Customizing Your Wallpaper
![customizing the wallpaper](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-wp-edit.png)

- **Edit with AI**: After generating your wallpaper, you can refine it using the chat-like prompt box. For example, type:
    - “Add a forest in the background.”
    - “Make the colors more pastel.”
    - “Can we try a more modern look?”

- **Other Editing Options**:
    - **Retry**: Not satisfied with the result? Regenerate the wallpaper with this option.
    - **AI Upscale 4x**: Increase the resolution for high-quality, large-format prints.
    - **AI Remove Background**: Create a clean, isolated wallpaper by removing the background.
    - **Change Style**: Explore different visual styles.
    - **Resize**: Adjust the wallpaper’s dimensions to fit your screen or wall perfectly.
    - **Undo/Redo**: Quickly go back or redo edits with these handy buttons.

### Step 5: Download or Share Your Wallpaper
![a cool wallpaper made with stockimg](https://media.stockimg.ai/template/image/ItgaGRy4_Z1Z.png)

- When you’re satisfied with the design, click **Download** to save your wallpaper. You can also use the **Share Link** feature to share your creation online.

### Extra Step: Use the Canvas

![sai canvas feature](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/sai-canvas.png)

- **Canvas**: For more control over your design, click on the **Canvas** feature under the **Image Generation** tab.

You can:

- **Add or Move Elements**: Position specific objects or designs on the canvas.
- **Layer Adjustments**: Manage layers for your design, bringing elements forward or sending them backward.
- **Custom Shapes & Text**: Insert custom shapes or add text to personalize your wallpaper even further.

- The canvas gives you complete flexibility to build a wallpaper from scratch or fine-tune an existing design.
