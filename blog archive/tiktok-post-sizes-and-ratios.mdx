## TikTok Video Post Sizes and Dimensions:

### TikTok Profile Picture Size:

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/tiktok-pp.png"
    alt="TikTok profile picture sizes and Ratios"
/>

- **Dimensions:** 200 x 200 pixels
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
- **File Format:** JPG or PNG

### TikTok In-Feed Videos:

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/tiktok-post.png"
    alt="TikTok In-Feed Videos sizes and Ratios"
/>

- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 pixels
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16
- **File Format:** MP4 or MOV

### TikTok Stories:

- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 pixels
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16
- **File Format:** MP4 or MOV

### TikTok Photo Posts:

- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 pixels
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (portrait), 1:1 (square), or 16:9 (landscape)
- **File Format:** JPG or PNG

## Frequently Asked Question (FAQs)

### What are the ideal dimensions for TikTok in-feed videos?

TikTok in-feed videos should have dimensions of 1080 x 1920 pixels with an aspect ratio of 9:16.

### Can I use landscape videos on TikTok?

While TikTok supports landscape videos, it's advisable to stick to portrait orientation for optimal viewing on mobile devices.

### What file formats are accepted for TikTok videos?

TikTok accepts MP4 and MOV file formats for videos.

### What size should my TikTok profile picture be?

The recommended size for TikTok profile pictures is 200 x 200 pixels with a 1:1 aspect ratio.

### Are there specific dimensions for TikTok stories?

Yes, TikTok stories should have dimensions of 1080 x 1920 pixels with an aspect ratio of 9:16.
