## Top Logo Types

![logo with thinker in center](https://media.stockimg.ai/image/e5t_HCsEc_Qv.png)

When designing your **logo**, it’s also important to consider the type of **logo** that best represents your brand. Here are the top types:

- **Wordmark**: A font-based **logo** focusing on the brand's name (e.g., Google, Coca-Cola).

- **Lettermark**: A typography-based **logo** consisting of initials (e.g., HBO, CNN).

- **Brandmark**: A symbol or icon representing the brand (e.g., Apple, Twitter).

- **Combination Mark**: Combines a symbol and a wordmark (e.g., Adidas, Doritos).

- **Emblem**: Text inside a symbol or icon (e.g., Starbucks, Harley-Davidson).
