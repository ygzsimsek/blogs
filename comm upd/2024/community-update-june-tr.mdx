### London Tech Week

![london week](https://media.stockimg.ai/static/solid/jun-london.jpg)

![london week2](https://media.stockimg.ai/static/solid/jun-london2.jpg)

> Sunumu izlemek için [buraya tıklayın](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=53C4VZfY5Eo&t=4s).

![uyzmz](https://media.stockimg.ai/static/solid/)

> [Üretken Yapay Zeka Mikro Zirvesi](https://kommunity.com/komunite/events/uretken-yz-mikro-zirvesi-0792090f).
